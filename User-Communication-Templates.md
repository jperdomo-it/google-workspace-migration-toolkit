# User Communication Templates

Clear communication is key to a successful migration. Use these templates to keep users informed and minimize support tickets during the transition.

## Template 1: The Announcement (Send 5 days before)
**Subject:** Important: We are moving to Google Workspace!

Hi Team,

To improve collaboration and security, we are migrating our email and calendar services to **Google Workspace** on **[Insert Date]**.

**What this means for you:**
* Your email address will remain the same.
* All your existing emails and folders will be migrated.
* You will gain access to Google Drive, Docs, and Meet.

No action is required from you at this moment. We will provide login instructions shortly.

---

## Template 2: Go-Live Instructions (Send 24 hours before)
**Subject:** Action Required: Your new Google Workspace account is ready

Hi [User Name],

Our migration to Google Workspace is happening tomorrow! 

**Login Details:**
* **URL:** [https://mail.google.com](https://mail.google.com)
* **Username:** [User Email]
* **Temporary Password:** [Insert Password]

**Important:** Upon your first login, you will be required to change your password and set up 2-Step Verification (2FA). 

Please save any work in your old [M365/Outlook] account by the end of today.

---

## Template 3: Post-Migration Support (Send on Day 1)
**Subject:** Welcome to Google Workspace - How to get help

Hi Team,

We are officially live on Google Workspace! 

If you have trouble finding your old emails or setting up your mobile device, please check our [Internal FAQ/Help Guide] or reply to this email for support.

Welcome to a more collaborative way of working!
